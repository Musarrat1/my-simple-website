Actress

Gigi Hadid - Jelena Noura Hadid
Diehard fans are aware that the globally famous supermodel has a stage name. While her birth name is Jelena, Hadid was nicknamed Gigi during her childhood, but exclusively by her family. In a 2015 interview, Gigi revealed that her mother used to be called by that name as a child, and she was given the same nickname around the house when she was young.
